1-1-22 - Welcome to FlickPick!

Welcome to FlickPick! FlickPick is currently an iOS app in development that will help users and their friends match on what movies they would want to watch! Simply like or dislike movies from your toggled streaming services, add your friends, and you can then see what you both like! Feel free to shuffle these matches to make picking even easier. Yes, thats right, Movie Night just got simple.
